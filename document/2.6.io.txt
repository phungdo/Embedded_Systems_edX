	External devices attached to uC provide functionality for the sys.
	An INPUT PORT is hardware on uC that allows info about external world to be entered in to computer.
	uC also has OUTPUT PORT to send info out.
	
	INTERFACE is defined as the collection of the I/O port, external electronics, physical devices, software.
 which combine to allow the computer to COMMUNICATE with world.
	
	LED - software turn light on and off. 
	Types of INTERFACE:
	+ PARALLEL - binary data availbe simultaneously on a group of lines
	+ SERIAL - _____________________ one bit at a time on a single line
	+ ANALOG - data are encoded as an voltage, current, power
	+ DIGITAL - __________________ a period, freq, pulse width, phase shift
